## Introduction

Tiny Wins ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application ("App").

This App is designed for **parents and guardians** to track and celebrate their children's positive behaviors and developmental milestones. We do not direct our services to children under 13, and children do not interact directly with our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:**
- Email address (if you create an account)
- Display name (optional)
- Apple ID token (if using Sign in with Apple, which may provide a private relay email)

**Child Profile Information (entered by parents):**
- Child's first name or nickname
- Child's age or birth year
- Avatar selection or emoji representation
- Color preferences for the child's profile

**Behavioral Data (entered by parents):**
- Behavior observations and logs
- Timestamps of logged behaviors
- Optional notes added to behavior entries
- Points and rewards tracking
- Goals and milestones

**Family Information:**
- Family name (optional)
- Co-parent invitations and connections

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device and Usage Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- General usage patterns (features used, session duration)
- Crash logs and performance data

**Analytics Data (with your consent):**
- Anonymized interaction events
- Feature engagement metrics
- Subscription status (anonymized tier information)

We do **NOT** automatically collect:
- Precise location data
- Contact lists
- Photos or media (unless you explicitly upload)
- Browsing history
- Data from other apps

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

**Authentication Providers:**
- If you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique identifier and, optionally, your email address (which may be a private relay address)

**Payment Processors:**
- Apple processes all subscription payments. We receive confirmation of subscription status but do NOT receive or store your payment card details, billing address, or financial information

## 3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

### 3.1 Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

Tiny Wins takes children's privacy extremely seriously. Our App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations.

### 3.2 App Designed for Parents, Not Children

- **Our App is designed for use by parents and guardians, not children**
- Children under 13 should NOT use this App directly
- All child-related data is entered and controlled by the parent/guardian
- Children do not create accounts, interact with the App, or provide any information directly

### 3.3 What Child Information Parents Enter

Parents may enter the following information about their children:
- First name or nickname (we recommend using nicknames)
- Age or birth year
- Behavioral observations
- Goals and milestones

### 3.4 How We Protect Child Information

- Child data is stored securely and encrypted in transit
- Child data is only accessible to the parent who entered it (and invited co-parents)
- We do NOT use child data for advertising or marketing
- We do NOT share child data with third parties for their marketing purposes
- We do NOT create public profiles for children
- Child data is never sold

### 3.5 Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:
- Review the child information you have entered
- Delete any or all child information at any time
- Refuse further collection of child information
- Export your child's data using Settings > "Export My Data"

To exercise these rights, use the in-app features or contact us at privacy@tinywins.app for assistance.

### 3.6 No Direct Collection from Children

We do NOT:
- Knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13
- Allow children to create accounts
- Enable children to post or share information publicly
- Use child data for behavioral advertising

If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal information directly from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

**Local Storage:**
- By default, your data is stored locally on your device
- Local data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)

**Cloud Storage (Optional):**
- If you sign in, your data may be synced to secure cloud servers
- Cloud data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure (Google Cloud Platform)
- Data centers are located in the United States
- Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
- **Cloud Data Encryption:** Data stored in our cloud infrastructure (Google Firebase) is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
- **Local Data Protection:** Data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, and iOS Data Protection)
- **Access Controls:** Strict access controls limit who can access user data
- **Authentication:** Secure authentication via Apple Sign-In with cryptographic verification
- **Monitoring:** Regular security monitoring and vulnerability assessments
- **Incident Response:** Procedures to detect, respond to, and recover from security incidents

### 4.3 Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:
- Keeping your device secure (using a passcode, keeping software updated)
- Protecting your account credentials
- Logging out of shared devices
- Not sharing your invite codes publicly

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to:
- Access all data you have entered in the App
- Export your data at any time using Settings > "Export My Data" (provides a JSON file with all your information)
- View what information we have about your account

### 7.2 Correction

You can correct or update any information directly within the App:
- Edit child profiles
- Modify behavior entries
- Update account settings

### 7.3 Deletion

You have the right to delete your data:
- **Delete Individual Items:** Remove specific behavior entries, children, or goals within the App
- **Delete All Data:** Use Settings > "Erase All Data" to remove all local and cloud data
- **Delete Account:** Contact us to delete your account and all associated data

### 7.4 Analytics Opt-Out

You can disable analytics collection:
- Go to Settings > Privacy
- Toggle off "Help Improve the App"
- This stops collection of anonymized usage data

### 7.5 Marketing Communications

We do not send marketing emails by default. If you have opted in to communications, you can opt out at any time.

### 7.6 Withdraw Consent

Where we rely on your consent to process data, you may withdraw consent at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

## 8. Data Retention

### 8.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

**Active Accounts:**
- We retain your data for as long as your account is active and you use our services
- Behavior logs and history are retained until you delete them

**Deleted Data:**
- When you delete data within the App, it is removed from our active systems within 30 days
- Backups may retain deleted data for up to 90 days before being purged

**Account Deletion:**
- Upon account deletion request, we will delete your data within 30 days
- Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., transaction records for tax purposes)

### 8.2 Inactive Accounts

- Accounts inactive for more than 24 months may be flagged for deletion
- We will attempt to notify you before deleting an inactive account
